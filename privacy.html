Privacy Policy

Information Collection

zanX OS collects minimal personal information necessary to provide our services. This includes your email address for authentication and synchronization of settings via Firebase.

Data Storage

Your data, including profile settings and preferences, is securely stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication). We do not store sensitive information locally after you log out.

Cookies

We use local storage and cookies strictly for session management and to remember your preferences (like wallpaper and theme).

Third-Party Services

zanX OS utilizes Firebase by Google. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@xancore.web.app.
